DIAMOND COLLECTION

2025 · Handcrafted in Vietnam

THE MKT STORY

At MKT, we believe that jewelry is not merely decoration — it is an extension of identity. Each grillz, each chain, each custom piece is born from a conversation between the wearer and the craftsman. We listen to your vision, translate it into gold and stone, and return to you something that is undeniably, irreversibly yours.

Our artisans in Ho Chi Minh City work with the same precision that defines the world's finest ateliers — yet we bring to it something no European house can replicate: the soul of Vietnamese craftsmanship, the rhythm of the street, the language of flex spoken fluently and authentically.

K'BOUTEGON

Every reflection emanating from a Diamond Grillz by MKT carries within it the full weight of intention. Hand-set stones, chosen for their fire and depth, are mounted in 18K gold structures built to last beyond the wearer.

SIGNATURE
GRILLZ

— Hand-set VS1 diamonds in solid 18K gold, individually chosen for fire and clarity.

— Custom sizing for upper and lower arches; fits crafted from dental-grade impressions.

— Fabrication time: 3 to 5 weeks per commission.

— Each piece signed and numbered by the maker.

CRAFTED
CONNECTIONS

The collection is created as a language of its own, where every line and detail tells a story of the bond between love and friendship. Without the need for explanation, the designs reflect companionship, understanding, and trust — values formed only through genuine appreciation.

Within each piece, love and friendship are inseparable, naturally intertwined just as they exist in life itself.

BOUND
TOGETHER

The image of the collection unfolds a story of two distinct individuals bound by an inseparable connection. The diamond-set metal circles interwoven between the two forms symbolize the intersection of friendship and love — independent, yet always drawn toward one another.

Rather than seeking perfect symmetry, the design celebrates a natural connection, where lasting value is formed through understanding and companionship.

The Maker

Creative Director

"To me, a diamond is not merely a rare material, but a memory crystallized through love and connection. Each creation in the Diamond Collection represents a bond that cannot be replicated — between people, between emotion and time. When love runs deep, there is no need for excess; only a timeless mark to remind us that we once belonged to one another, and always will."

Material Excellence

ETERNAL STANDARDS

Craftsmanship

Every MKT piece is shaped by hands that have spent years refining the art of precision. Each joint, each setting, each surface is worked to a standard that machines cannot replicate — because the mark of a craftsman is present in every detail.

18K Gold

18K gold is chosen as the foundation of every design — achieving the perfect balance between durability, refined golden brilliance, and exceptional craftsmanship. It is the material through which MKT's vision becomes permanent.

Natural Diamond

Paired with meticulously selected natural diamonds, each creation reflects pure light and timeless value. Stones are chosen individually for fire, clarity, and the way they carry meaning — because nothing less is worthy of the wearer.

BUILT FOR
THOSE WHO
DEMAND MORE

Every MKT piece begins not with metal, but with a conversation. Our craftsmen in Ho Chi Minh City work at the intersection of traditional Vietnamese goldsmithing and contemporary street culture — producing grillz and jewelry that carry the weight of intention, precision, and identity.

No shortcuts. No compromises. Only work that stands as a testament to the wearer's ambition.

From the first sketch to the final polish, every MKT creation passes through the hands of a single craftsman — ensuring that each piece carries one consistent vision. We reject the factory. We reject the template. What you receive is not a product; it is an original.

This is what it means to wear MKT.
