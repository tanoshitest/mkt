About the Collection

M'RISING STAR

M'RISING STAR brings pigment into the MKT universe. Hand-applied enamel, colored gemstone settings, and vivid PVD coating combine to create grillz that don't just catch light — they throw it.

The Color Edition started as an experiment and became a movement. Clients who wanted something beyond gold and silver got a palette. Each combination is approved and built around the wearer's aesthetic — never generic, never repeated.

Material18K Gold · PVD Color Coating · Enamel
ProcessColor consultation · Hand-applied enamel · 10–16 day production
EditionColor palette approved per client — each combination is exclusive
