The Founder

Mai Kim Thanh
Master Craftsman

As the founder of MKT, grillz are not merely a piece of jewelry for showing off or chasing trends. To me, grillz are a personal language — a refined medium through which one's individuality, life story, and inner spirit are expressed with depth and intention.

"MKT exists to elevate grillz beyond mere display, transforming them into an integral part of fashion, culture, and personal storytelling — a space where the wearer doesn't just shine, but truly belongs."

— Mai Kim Thanh
Our Vision

Identity,
not imitation.

No remakes, no mass production. Each piece is a personal statement crafted with artistic vision and elevated standards of fine craftsmanship — designed for those who understand that true luxury is singular.

01
Story Telling

Every detail is purposeful. We craft through a deep understanding of the person behind the smile — their style, journey, and irreplaceable personal mark.

02
Meticulous Craft

Anglage by hand. Burnished pivots. Each surface treated with obsessive precision — the same standards found in the finest Swiss ateliers.

03
Unique Masterpiece
