The Movement and the Materials

The RM 07-01 represents a paradigm of horological engineering — where ceramic material science meets haute joaillerie. Each component is conceived, engineered, and assembled by hand, ensuring that no two timepieces are ever identical. The result is a watch that transcends functionality to become a wearable work of art.

01

Geometric Dial Design

The open-worked dial features a multi-layered geometric pattern — rectangles, diamonds — in a vibrant palette of orange, blue, and teal. Set with several distinct diamonds, meticulously placed to add a sparkle like eternal snowflakes caught in perpetual motion.

02

Coloured Ceramic Case

The stunning TZP-P pale lavender pink ceramic case is a masterclass in material engineering. It provides incredible hardness, scratch resistance, and a unique velvety touch — contrasting beautifully with the polished metal elements that frame the movement.

03

CRMA1 Calibre Movement

Powering the watch is the in-house automatic CRMA1 calibre — an open-work movement visible from the angled view. It showcases complex bridge architecture and high-end finishing, including a variable geometry rotor for optimized winding efficiency.
