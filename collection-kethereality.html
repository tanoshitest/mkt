About the Collection

K'ETHEREALITY

K'ETHEREALITY explores the space between material and immaterial. Openwork structures, translucent stone inlays, and whisper-thin metal sections create jewelry that seems to float — barely there, yet utterly present.

Designed to challenge the weight of traditional fine jewelry, Ethereality pieces feel like air on the skin. The engineering required to achieve this lightness is invisible by design — the craft disappears so the concept remains.

Material18K White Gold · Sapphire · Rose Quartz
ProcessOpenwork construction · Stone inlay · 14–21 day production
EditionPart of the Q4/2025 limited jewelry release
